Catering

Simple Menu:

Opt for a buffet-style meal with a limited menu. Focus on affordable yet delicious dishes, such as pasta, rice, salads, and a few meat options.

Potluck Style:

If your family and friends are willing, consider a potluck-style reception where guests bring a dish to share. This can save a significant amount on catering costs.

Cake:

Instead of an elaborate wedding cake, consider a smaller cake for cutting and a sheet cake for serving guests.
