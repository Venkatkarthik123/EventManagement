Catering

Buffet or Family Style:

Choose a buffet or family-style meal to encourage sharing and interaction. This can be more cost-effective than plated meals.

Menu Options:

A diverse menu featuring appetizers, main courses, desserts, and beverages, catering to various dietary preferences (vegetarian, non-vegetarian, vegan, etc.).

Staffing:

Professional catering staff to assist with serving and ensuring a smooth dining experience.

Tasting Session:

Pre-event tasting session to finalize menu selections.

Cake:

Invest in a modestly sized wedding cake for display and cutting, supplemented by a larger sheet cake for serving guests.
