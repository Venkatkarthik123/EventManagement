Decorations

Themed Decor:

Choose a theme that reflects your personality and faith, using decorations that can be reused or repurposed after the wedding.

Floral Arrangements:

Use seasonal flowers and greenery for centerpieces and bouquets. Consider DIY arrangements or hiring a local florist who can work within your budget.

Lighting:

Enhance the ambiance with string lights or candles, which can create a romantic atmosphere without high costs.
