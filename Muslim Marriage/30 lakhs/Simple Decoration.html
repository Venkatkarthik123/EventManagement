Decoration

Floral Arrangements:

Use fresh or artificial flowers to create centerpieces and backdrops. Opt for seasonal blooms to save costs.

String Lights:

Hang fairy lights or lanterns to create a warm and inviting atmosphere, especially for evening events.

DIY Decor:

Get creative with DIY projects like handmade banners, table runners, or photo backdrops using fabric or paper.

Simple Stage Setup:

Create a focal point for the ceremony with a simple backdrop of fabric or flowers, separating the male and female sections.

Personal Touches:

Include family photos or meaningful items in the decor to add a personal touch to the celebration.
