Sangeet

Description:

The Sangeet is a vibrant pre-wedding celebration that brings together family and friends for an evening of music, dance, and entertainment. Traditionally, this event is characterized by performances from both sides of the family, where they showcase their talents through song and dance. The Sangeet is a time for laughter, joy, and bonding, often featuring choreographed dances, live music, and sometimes even professional performers. It sets a festive mood leading up to the wedding day.
