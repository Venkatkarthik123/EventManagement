Haldi Cermony

Description:

The Haldi ceremony is a joyful and colorful pre-wedding ritual where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom. This ceremony is believed to purify the couple and enhance their beauty before the wedding. Family and friends gather to participate in this fun event, often resulting in playful moments filled with laughter and joy. The bright yellow color of the turmeric symbolizes prosperity and fertility, making it an essential part of the wedding celebrations..
