Ganesh Pooja

Description:

The Ganesh Pooja is a traditional Hindu ritual performed to invoke the blessings of Lord Ganesh, the remover of obstacles, before the wedding festivities begin. This ceremony is often held at the bride's home and involves offerings of flowers, fruits, and sweets to the deity. Family members gather to participate in prayers and rituals, creating a spiritual atmosphere that sets a positive tone for the upcoming celebrations. The Ganesh Pooja symbolizes the couple's desire for a smooth and prosperous marriage.
