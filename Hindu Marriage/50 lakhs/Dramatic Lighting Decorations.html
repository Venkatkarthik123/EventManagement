Dramatic Lighting Decoration

Uplighting:

Placing lights on the floor and directing them upwards can create a warm glow and highlight walls, columns, or other architectural elements.

String Lights:

Twinkling fairy lights can add a whimsical touch, perfect for outdoor or rustic-themed events.

Spotlighting:

Focused lights can be used to draw attention to specific areas, such as the cake table or the dance floor.

Color Customization:

Lighting can be adjusted to match the event's color scheme, creating a cohesive look throughout the venue.
