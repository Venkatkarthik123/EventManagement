Homam

Description:

After the Kanyadaan takes place, the sacred fire is lit. The couple praises the Agni Devta, the Lord of Fire, in hopes of bringing the presence of Vishnu onto the ceremony. The Vivaah Homa is performed in order to indicate the purity of the upcoming rituals, all conducted with the help of the fire.

Homam is a sacred fire ritual performed during Hindu weddings to invoke blessings from the gods. The couple, along with priests, participates in this ceremony, offering prayers and offerings into the sacred fire. The Homam symbolizes the couple's commitment to each other and their shared journey ahead. It is believed that the fire acts as a witness to their vows and promises, making it a significant part of the wedding ceremony.
