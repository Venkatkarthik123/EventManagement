Engagement

Description:

The engagement ceremony, known as "Sagai" or "Mangni" in Hindu culture, is a significant pre-wedding ritual that marks the formal commitment between the bride and groom. This ceremony is often celebrated with family and friends and serves as a precursor to the wedding.

The engagement ceremony is a beautiful and meaningful event in Hindu marriages, symbolizing the couple's commitment to each other and their families. It serves as a joyful occasion for both families to come together, celebrate, and prepare for the upcoming wedding. By honoring traditions and involving loved ones, the engagement sets a positive tone for the marriage journey ahead.
