Ganesh Pooja

Description:

Ganesh Puja is a significant ritual in Hindu weddings, performed to seek the blessings of Lord Ganesh for a smooth and obstacle-free marriage. Key rituals include invoking Ganesh, offering modaks (sweets), reciting mantras, and performing aarti, typically conducted by the bride's family a day before or on the wedding day itself.

Ganesh Puja is a vital ritual in Hindu weddings, symbolizing the couple's commitment to starting their life together with divine blessings. It not only marks the beginning of the wedding festivities but also reinforces the cultural values of family unity and spiritual well-being.
