Homam

Description:

Homam, or Havan, is a sacred fire ritual in Hindu marriage ceremonies that symbolizes purity and the invocation of divine blessings. It involves offerings to Agni, the fire god, who serves as a witness to the marriage, ensuring the couple's happiness, prosperity, and the sanctity of their union.

Homam is a vital component of Hindu marriage ceremonies, embodying the spiritual essence of the union. It not only marks the beginning of the couple's journey together but also emphasizes the importance of divine blessings and cultural traditions in their married life.
